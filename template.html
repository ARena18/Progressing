Movie Database

Team Members: Anna Muller, Rena Ahn, and Tina Nguyen

Relations:
  1. USER_ACCOUNT
  2. MOVIE
  3. WATCHLISTMOVIE
  4. USER_RATING
  5. ARTIST
Queries:
  1. Movie Rating
    Finds and returns the average rating for each movie. If the movie has not received a rating yet, its rating will be empty.
  2. Movie Count
    Finds and returns the number of movies each account has in their watchlist.
  3. Popular Movies
    Finds and returns the ID, title, and genre of the movies that are added to watchlists the most.
  4. Movie Reviews
    Finds and lists all reviews for each movie and the name of the reviewer.
  5. Unpopular Movies
    Finds movies that have not been added to a watchlist and returns all of its properties.
